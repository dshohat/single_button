=== Emergency Alerts - Test Alarm ===

After the device is connected to WiFi and running, you can test the alarm in two ways:

1. Via the Web UI:
   - Browse to the device IP (shown on OLED)
   - Click "Trigger Test Alarm" button
   - The siren will sound and the OLED will flash "!! ALERT !!"
   - Press the physical button to silence, or click "Stop Test Alarm" on the web page

2. Via command line (replace IP with your device's IP):
   - Trigger test alarm:
     curl -X POST http://DEVICE_IP/test
   - Stop test alarm:
     curl -X POST http://DEVICE_IP/clear_test
   - PowerShell alternative:
     Invoke-WebRequest -Method POST -Uri http://DEVICE_IP/test
     Invoke-WebRequest -Method POST -Uri http://DEVICE_IP/clear_test

=== Files to upload to device ===
Upload these files from mine\code\ to the ESP32:
  - emergency_alerts.py
  - areas.json        (read-only reference: all areas->cities)
  - area_names.json   (lightweight list of area names only)
  - ssd1306.py
  - boot.py (should contain: execfile("emergency_alerts.py"))

Config files (auto-created on device, do NOT upload):
  - wifi_config.json   (saved WiFi networks)
  - alert_areas.json   (selected areas/cities to monitor)
